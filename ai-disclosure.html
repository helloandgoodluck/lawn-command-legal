AI Disclosure

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Lawn Command uses artificial intelligence to help you take better care of your lawn. This page explains exactly how AI is used, what data is involved, and what it means for you.

AI Provider

All AI features are powered by Google's Gemini API. When you use an AI feature, data is sent to Google's servers for processing and a response is returned to the App. We do not operate our own AI models.

Your Control

AI features are entirely optional. If you prefer not to use them:

  • Turf-Check: Simply don't take a scan. No photos are sent unless you explicitly initiate a scan.
  • Coach's Note: The daily summary generates automatically, but no personal data is included in the request.
